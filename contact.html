Frequently Asked Questions

How do I contact NyvoraAI? +

Email us at nyvora.ai@outlook.com or use the contact form above. We reply within 1–2 business days. For urgent matters, write "URGENT" in the subject line.

Can I write for NyvoraAI? +

Absolutely. We welcome writers who are passionate about AI. Select "Write for Us" in the form above and tell us about yourself. No prior publishing experience is required — just clarity and curiosity.

Does NyvoraAI accept advertising? +

Yes. We partner with brands looking to reach an AI-focused audience. Select "Advertising Partnership" in the contact form and we will send you our media kit with rates and details.

How do I submit an AI news tip? +

Use the contact form above and choose "Story Tip / News Submission". Include a brief description of the story, a source link if available, and why it matters to the AI community.
