# NyvoraAI

NyvoraAIis an independent, no-paywall AI news and education platform covering large language models, AI agents, tools, safety, and research. Built and maintained by [Varun Lalwani](https://nyvoraai.github.io).

🌐 **Live site:** [nyvoraai.github.io](https://nyvoraai.github.io)

---

## About

NyvoraAI publishes free, in-depth guides and news on:

- 🤖 Large Language Models (LLMs)
- 🛡️ AI Safety
- 🔬 AI Research
- 🧰 AI Tools & Reviews
- 💼 AI in Business
- 🦾 Robotics

Our mission is to make AI education accessible to everyone — no paywalls, no gatekeeping.

## Tech Stack

- Static site hosted on GitHub Pages
- HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript
- Dark, space-themed design system (animated star fields, auras, scanlines, grid overlays)
- Fonts: Space Grotesk, Source Serif 4, JetBrains Mono
- Google Tag Manager for analytics
- Formspree for contact and newsletter forms
- JSON-LD structured data (Article, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList schemas) for SEO/GEO/AEO

## Site Structure

```
├── index.html              # Homepage
├── ai-news.html             # AI news hub with category filtering & search
├── blog.html                # Blog article hub
├── blog/                    # Individual blog posts
├── ai-news/                 # Individual AI news articles
├── about.html                # About page
├── contact.html              # Contact page (Formspree-powered)
├── sitemap.xml                # XML sitemap
└── assets/                    # Images, CSS, JS, favicon
```

## Content Categories

Articles are organized into: LLMs, AI Safety, AI Research, AI Tools, Business, and Robotics — each with categorized, searchable, filterable cards on the AI News hub.
